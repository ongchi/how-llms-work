The Transformer Loop

1 Tokenize "to be or not to..." → [51, 230, 36, 47, ...]
2 Embedding numerical representations transformed into high-dimensional vectors
3 Attention context-aware vectors; Sliding Window Attention (SWA)
4 Logits linear projection → raw score per vocabulary
5 Softmax(T) scores → probability distribution
6 Sample pick next token → go to step 1
